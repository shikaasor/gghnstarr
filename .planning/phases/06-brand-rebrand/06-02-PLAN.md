  <files>app/components/layout/Header.tsx, app/components/layout/Footer.tsx</files>
  <action>
**Header.tsx changes:**

1. Add `import Image from 'next/image';` at the top (after the existing imports).

2. Replace the entire `{/* Logo */}` block:
```tsx
{/* Logo */}
<Link
  href="/"
  className="font-serif font-bold text-lg text-teal-400 hover:text-teal-300 transition-colors"
>
  GGHN STARR
</Link>
```
with:
```tsx
{/* Logo */}
<Link href="/" className="flex items-center shrink-0">
  <Image
    src="/amr-logo.jpeg"
    alt="AntiMicrobial Resistance Initiative"
    width={160}
    height={64}
    priority
    className="object-contain h-12 w-auto"
    style={{ mixBlendMode: 'multiply' }}
  />
</Link>
```

The `mixBlendMode: 'multiply'` in the inline style removes the white JPEG background on the dark green header. `priority` ensures the logo loads without LCP delay. `shrink-0` prevents the logo from being compressed on narrow screens.

**Footer.tsx changes:**

<p className="text-slate-300 text-sm mt-1">
