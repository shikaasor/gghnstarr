    Heading: "Bayesian Hierarchical Forecasting"
    Content: "Bayesian methods are central to STARR's approach because they explicitly quantify uncertainty — a critical requirement when advising policy under incomplete data conditions common across African health systems. Bayesian hierarchical models combine evidence from multiple countries and surveillance sources, producing probabilistic forecasts rather than false-precision point estimates."

    Paragraph 2: "STARR uses ABMs to stress-test policy interventions at a granular level, simulating how a stewardship programme would propagate through a network of interconnected facilities and communities under different compliance assumptions.
